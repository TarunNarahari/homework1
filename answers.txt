
What is a remote repository?

A remote repository is where parts of a project that one has created can be
be hosted. This includes on a network or on the internet.


What is the difference between a merge and a rebase?

In general, a rebase commits on top of another branch. On the other hand, a
merge joins to developmental branches together. Hence, a merge preserves the 
history of what came before it while a rebase rewrites what came before.
